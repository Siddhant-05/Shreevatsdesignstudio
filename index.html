The Studio

Architecture as
Lived Experience

Sacred Space · Timeless Craft

Shreevats was founded on a singular conviction — that architecture is not merely built matter, but the art of shaping consciousness itself. Our practice draws from the profound visual grammar of Indian temple architecture, Mughal spatial geometry, and Maratha ornamental legacy.

Every project begins with deep listening: to the land, the light, the client's aspirations, and the cultural memory embedded in the site. We design spaces that breathe, spaces that remember, spaces that endure across generations.

"Architecture is frozen music. Indian architecture is the music of eternity — each stone placed not by accident, but by cosmic intention."
— Studio Philosophy

Our Creed

We do not build Structures. We compose sacred silences.

01 / Vastu
Cosmic Alignment

Every project begins with the ancient science of Vastu Shastra — reading the land's energy, aligning form with cardinal directions, and harmonizing built space with the rhythms of nature.

02 / Yantra
Sacred Geometry

We embed the mathematical language of the universe — golden proportions, mandala grids, temple geometries that subconsciously calm the mind and elevate the spirit of all who inhabit the space.

03 / Shilpa
Master Craftsmanship

Drawing from the Shilpa Shastra tradition, we collaborate with hereditary craftspeople — stone carvers, metal workers, and fresco painters — so every surface carries the memory of skilled hands.

How We Work

The Shreevats
Method

01
Site Reading & Vastu Survey

We begin by listening to the land. Deep analysis of topography, solar orientation, water flow, and cultural context before a single line is drawn.

02
Mandala Concept & Sacred Grid

The spatial concept develops through sacred geometry — establishing the cosmic center, directional axes, and proportion system that governs the entire design.

03
Material Curation & Craft Selection

Indigenous materials sourced with intention. We engage hereditary artisans — stone carvers, jaali craftspeople, fresco painters — to embed cultural memory into surfaces.

04
Construction & Consecration

Construction follows traditional building sequences. Key structural milestones are marked with quiet ceremony, acknowledging the transformation of earth into shelter.

05
Handover & Living Guidance

We offer seasonal maintenance guidance and a living document — the architectural memory of the space — so its wisdom is preserved across generations.
